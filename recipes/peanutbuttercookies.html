Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Craving for some delicious peanut butter cookies? Here's a simple and easy recipe for you to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients togehter
  2. Beat the peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth; beat in the eggs afterwards.
  3. Sift the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt into a separate bowl; stir into butter mixture until dough is combined. Put into the refrigerator for 1 hour to chill cookie dough.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees (190 degrees). Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them 2-inch apart onto an ungreased baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork with a crisscross pattern.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 7 to 10 minutes until they're golden.
  6. Let the cookies cool completely before removing them. After they're finished cooling, enjoy!
