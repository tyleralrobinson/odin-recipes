Lasagna

Description

Here's a simple and easy Lasagna recipe you can make for you, your family, or friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 (32 oz) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of dried parsley
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • ground black pepper
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 pounds of cottage cheese

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the ground beef in the skillet for 8 to 10 minutes until it's browned and crumbly. Drain and discard the grease. Stir the spahgetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt and pepper into a large bowl.
  4. Spread the 3/4 cup of sauce into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce; repeat the layers once more. Top with the remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese. Pour 1/2 cup of water along the edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Put it inside the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an addtional 10 minutes then let it stand for 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve and enjoy the dish!
