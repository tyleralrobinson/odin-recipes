Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Need a dessert to go along with your dinner or have as a snack? Here's an easy recipe for you to make it from scratch.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 tbsp. unsalted butter, melted, divided
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp. of white sugar
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 3 tbsp. unsalted softened butter
  • 2/3 cup of whole milk

Filling

  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp. ground cinnamon

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 4 oz softened cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup softened unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degress C). Use a square 8-inch brush baking dish with 1tbsp. of melted butter.
  2. Whisk flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Hand mix 3 tbsp. soften butter into the flour mixture until the butter is lentil-sized.
  3. Pour milk into the flour-butter mixture and stir with a rubber spatula until it's a soft dough.
  4. Dough out onto a well-floured work surface and roll the dough into a 10-inch square pan. Brush along the surface of the dough with the remaining 2 tbsp. melted butter.
  5. For the filling, whisk 1/4 cup of white sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon together in a small bowl. Sprinkle 1 tbsp. of the cinnamon-sugar mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon sugar over butter-brushed dough.
  6. Roll the dough around filling to form a log; cut the log into 9 rolls about 1-inch wide; place rolls cut-side up in the prepared baking dish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, they'll be done once the rolls are puffed and lightly golden.
  8. For the frosting, beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, 1/4 cup softened butter, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until it's smooth.
  9. Glaze the top of the cinnamon rolls with the frosting and you're done.
